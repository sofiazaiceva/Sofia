dominant wavelength 600 nm

performance with the SuperCollider programming platform and synthesisers. The "dominant wavelength 600 nm" symbolises the orange colour and the increase of the consciousness of the Sacred Chakra. Its aim is to allow our emotions to flow freely and restore balance by evoking their expression and physical reactivity in an experimental meditative sound practice. The premiere took place at the COMMUTE #5 festival in Tallinn, Estonia.
